Stage 5 of 5 — Call & Close
Questions 22–27

The close stage reveals whether your conversion problem is systemic (upstream) or structural (in the call itself). Most coaches who get this far have one of three issues: an unqualified lead, a weak call structure, or a follow-up gap post-call.
